Enterprise Roadmap

- Enterprise SSO + SCIM Provisioning - District-grade identity management with single sign-on, automated user provisioning/deprovisioning, role mapping, and full auditability.
- Privacy & Compliance Admin Center - Built-in GDPR/FERPA-ready workflows for data export/delete requests, retention controls, legal holds, and trust documentation.
- Reliability, SLA & Procurement Toolkit - Status transparency, SLA-ready support routing, and procurement-friendly PO/invoicing workflows with district rollups and usage caps.
- Assessment Integrity Suite - Defensible oral assessment with follow-up questions, prompt/rubric locking and versioning, evidence trails, and score revision history.
- District Rubric Library + Calibration - Standardize scoring at scale with district rubric templates, exemplars, calibration sessions, and inter-rater reliability reporting.
- Leadership Reporting Suite - School and district reporting that rolls up standards mastery, growth over time, coverage gaps, and actionable intervention lists.
- Longitudinal Student Portfolio - A multi-year oral evidence portfolio with curated "best work," optional reflections, and conference-ready export packs.
- Core Integrations Suite - Rostering (Clever/ClassLink), LMS distribution (Classroom/Teams/Canvas), SIS-ready exports, and optional district data webhooks.
- Teacher Workflow Polish - Faster scoring and feedback with snippet libraries, voice feedback, smart sampling for coaching/QA, and streamlined exception handling.
- Student Experience Polish - Practice mode, exemplars, mobile-first recording, mic checks, and resilient uploads that reduce failed submissions.
- Enterprise Operations Toolkit - Guided rollout (pilot to scale) with onboarding wizards, implementation health dashboards, diagnostics, and support-ready runbooks.
